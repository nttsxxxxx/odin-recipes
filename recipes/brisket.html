Brisket

Texas style brisket, originating from Ashkenazi jews who immigrated to texas is packed with flavor with only a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 pounds beef brisket
  • salt (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F, or 160°C
  2. Season both sides of brisket with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Place in a Dutch oven or a large roasting pan with a cover.
  4. Cook in a preheated oven, covered, with no water for 3 hours.
  5. Remove from pan and slice.
  6. Return the slices to the pan and mix the BBQ sauce with water in a seperate dish.
  7. Pour the mixture over your sliced brisket.
  8. Cover your pan and cook for another hour.
  9. Wait for brisket to cool, and serve.
