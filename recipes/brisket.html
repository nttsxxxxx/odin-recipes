Brisket

Texas style brisket, coming from Ashkenazi jews who immigrated to texas, is me and my families love and life! And should be yours too!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 pounds beef brisket
  • salt (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F, or 160°C
  2. Season both sides of brisket with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Place in a Dutch oven or a large roasting pan with a cover.
  3. Cook in a preheated oven, covered, with no water for 3 hours.
  4. Remove from pan and slice. Return the slices to the pan. Mix the BBQ sauce with water and pour over your sliced brisket. Cover your pan and cook for another hour.
