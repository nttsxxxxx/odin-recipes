Beef Stroganov

Beef Stroganov, named after either Pavel or Alexander Stroganov, take your guess. But you can't think on an empty stomach!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 and a half tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of salt
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 and 3 quarters of a cup of milk
  • 1 and a quarter cup of beef stock
  • An 8 ounce package of egg noodles
  • A half cup of sour cream
  • A quarter cup of fresh (grated) Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the beef in the skillet until it's browned and crumbly (5 to 7 minutes) then drain.
  2. In a small bowl, add in the cornstarch, salt, parsley, garlic powder, and the onion powder.
  3. Pour the small bowl, milk, beef stock, and noodles into the skillet with the beef, bring to a boil, cover and reduce the heat to a simmer. Stir occaisonally until the noodles are tender (~5 minutes), stir in the sour cream and Parmesan cheese until combined
